Research Experience

    Department of Biomedical Engineering, Zhejiang University                 Ph.D. candidate 2016 - Pres.

    Optical biosensor of molybdenum disulfide (MoS2) with gold nanoparticles for explosive detection (The first auther)

  • Researched about MoS2 and designed the biosensor

  • Synthesized the composites of MoS2 with gold nanoparticles and evaluated the composites with transmission electron microscopy (TEM), scanning electron microscope (SEM), Raman spectra

  • Functionalized the nanocomposites with specially-designed peptides

  • Experimented with the biosensor to detect explosives by absorption spectra

  • Processed data with several softwares and wrote the paper

    Nanoplasmonic biosensor coupling electrochemistry to monitor heavy metal ions with nanocone-arrays (preparing independently)

  • Studied about localized surface plasmon resonance (LSPR) of nanocone arrays

  • Designed specific DNA strands that were sensitive to heavy mental ions

  • Assembled DNA with nanocone-arrays and used the biosensor to detect Hg2+ through LSPR spectroscopy

  • Used electrochemical methods (differential pulse voltammetry and square wave voltammetry) coupling with LSPR for Hg2+ detection to get higher sensitivity
